Task management, scheduling, and workflow orchestration

// Overview

The Delegation module manages tasks, schedules, and reminders. ADAM helps you break down complex projects, track progress, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

ACTIVE

// Capabilities

  • Create and track tasks with deadlines
  • Break down complex projects
  • Set reminders and notifications
  • Schedule meetings and events
  • Prioritize with urgency/importance matrix
  • Progress tracking and reporting

// Example Usage

> remind: meeting tomorrow at 3pm > task: break down "build website" into subtasks > schedule: team call for Friday 2pm > list: all tasks due this week > priority: reschedule by importance
