🎵 SPOTIFY

Control playback, search tracks, and manage playlists

Home / Plugins / Spotify

// Overview

The Spotify plugin allows you to control your music playback. Play, pause, skip tracks, search for songs, and manage your playlists directly with ADAM.

ACTIVE

// Capabilities

  • Play, pause, and skip tracks
  • Search for songs, artists, and albums
  • Manage playlists (create, add, remove)
  • Get currently playing track info
  • Control volume

// Example Usage

> spotify: play "Bohemian Rhapsody" > spotify: pause music > spotify: next song > spotify: search for artist "Queen" > spotify: add "Like a Rolling Stone" to "My Playlist"
